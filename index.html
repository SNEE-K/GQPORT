Las Vegas, NV

I'm a multidisciplinary creative director and product designer with 15+ years spanning brand identity, UX strategy, campaign design, motion graphics, and entertainment marketing. I've led visual direction for live productions at Planet Hollywood and Allegiant Stadium, shipped digital products used by thousands of daily visitors, and built brand systems from zero for artists, athletes, and startups.

M.S. in UX Research & Design, Maryland Institute College of Art. B.A. in Graphic Design, University of West Florida. Formerly a Cryptologic Technician in the US. Navy, with TS/Q clearances — the attention to detail doesn't turn off.

Based in Las Vegas. Open to mid to senior creative and product design roles, creative direction opportunities, and select freelance work.
